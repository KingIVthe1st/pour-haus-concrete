About

Blood & Aggregate.
Two generations deep.

Services

The Pour

01

Domestic

The ground your children will inherit. Foundations poured at 4,000 PSI with fiber mesh reinforcement. Driveways that shrug off Atlanta's freeze-thaw cycles. Patios smooth enough to walk barefoot, strong enough to hold a lifetime of Sunday dinners.

02

Industrial

Warehouse floors that absorb ten-ton loads without complaint. Parking structures engineered for decades of abuse. We pour slabs that machinery can't crack, surfaces that forklifts can't scar. Commercial scale, surgical precision.

03

Bespoke

Exposed aggregate revealing river stone and memory. Acid-stained surfaces that bloom like watercolors. Stamped patterns pressed into wet cement by hand—not machine. Concrete as art, signed by craftsmen who understand the chemistry of beauty.

04

Renewal

Cracks tell stories of movement and time. We listen, then repair. Spalling surfaces reborn through polymer-modified overlays. Settlement corrected with precision lifting. We don't just patch—we restore structural integrity to what previous hands built.

The Process

01

Survey

We walk your soil. Feel the grade. Test the clay. Every pour begins with understanding what lies beneath—Georgia earth has stories to tell, and we know how to listen.

02

Pour

6AM starts. Steam rising off fresh mix. Forms aligned to the millimeter. Bull floats and magnesium screeds in practiced hands—this is where forty years of muscle memory meets modern precision.

03

Cure

Patience cannot be rushed. 28 days to reach full strength. We wet-cure, cover, protect. Time is the final ingredient—the difference between concrete that cracks and concrete that becomes monument.
